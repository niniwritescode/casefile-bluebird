## CASE SUMMARY

Over the last 18 months, five wealthy businessmen have been found murdered across the city.

Common patterns observed in all murders:

1. Murder happens ONLY during heavy rainfall.
2. Victims are always influential businessmen.
3. Cause of death: strangulation.
4. Rope used in every murder has the exact same rare knot style.
5. A handwritten note saying "BLUEBIRD" is left near every body.
6. Wet footprints are found at every crime scene.
7. Each footprint contains a star-shaped mark in the center of the sole.

Police believe the same killer is responsible.
